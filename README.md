# Hi there, I'm Princess Imelda Odato 👋

I am a Computer Scientist by training and an aspiring Machine Learning Engineer. Everything in and around us is data, our processes and systems are built around data, our world is data, and that is what I am deeply passionate about. The world as data. Paraphrasing one of my favourite authors, Professor Gussai Sheikheldin, we need to start and continue building technological solutions for african problems and to liberate africans. That is what I enjoy taking part in and what I strive to do as a developer working to grow my skills. 

- 🔭 **Current Focus:** My current project focuses on building an automated flood alert system, as well as including a flood prediction model on the back-end to facilitate forecasting of flood weather patterns.
- 🌱 **Learning & Exploration:** I am primarily working with Python, allowing me to build a CNN and work with ResNet. I am also building the solution with a focus on Notification APIs and SMS Gateways.
- ⚡ **Core Interests:** Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud Infrastructure
